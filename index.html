A Community Media Initiative

I’m Donovan Wilson, a Tacoma‑based film writer, director, and producer. I’m offering free video services to nonprofits, low‑income individuals, bands & musicians, and contemporary artists who might not otherwise access professional production. The goal is dignified, high‑impact visuals—short promos, performance cuts, artist profiles, and narrative shorts—delivered in a clean, cinematic style.

Nonprofits & Community

Mission highlights, fundraising teasers, event recaps, staff/volunteer portraits.

Bands & Musicians

10–30s promos, live session cuts, music teasers with fashion‑forward styling.

Artists & Creators

Studio vignettes, process reels, exhibition trailers, dignified portraits.

Narrative Stories

Director/producer support to bring short scripts to screen in a minimalist, elegant way.

Availability is limited and projects are selected based on alignment and impact. With permission, work may be featured in my portfolio.

How it works

  1. Send a short note about your project and timeline.
  2. Quick call to align on vision and logistics.
  3. Plan a lean shoot (10–60 minutes on‑site is common).
  4. Deliver edit‑ready clips or a finished micro‑promo.
